Internet: A Primer


Definition.

The Internet is a system of interconnected networks allthroughout the world.

This allows information from any part of the world to be transmitting and received by computer systems regardless of distance or high time-latencies

Cool Facts

NeXT Computer

The World Wide Web was first ran on TimBL's NeXT Computer

Web3

Web3 is the next evolution for the internet, allowing decentralised economics using blockchain. Examples include cryptocurrency and NFTs

Internet of Things

IoT refer to smart devices that use internet communications especially WiFi to send and receive data. This may include smart home appliances, sensors, locks which is viewable on their internet apps
