circle



Definition. A circle is a perfect rounded shape that is not bounded by any fixed points by its angles. Therefore it should make a perfect 360°

Some properties

  • It is the only shape to not have more than one edge or vertice;
  • The formula for calculating its area is πr^2
  • The formula for calculating its circumference is 2πr
