Rhombus


Definition A rhombus is a convex quadrilateral with four equal sides.

Some properties

  • All sides are equal;
  • Opposite sides are parallel;
  • Opposite angles are equal;
  • Diagonals bisect each other at right angles;
  • Diagonals bisect the angels of the rhombus
