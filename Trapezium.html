Trapezium



Definition. A trapezium is a quadrilateral with at least one pair of opposite sides parallel.

Area of a Trapezium

  • The parallel sides are called the bases;

  • The other two sides are called the legs;

  • The distance between the two bases is called the altitude;

  • The area is the average of the two base lengths times the altitude
